Beef Tacos

Beef Tacos.

30 min eady to follow recipe

I'm always looking for an easy recipe to make, if you are a busy body this recipe is for you!

Ingredients
  • 1 pound lean groud beef
  • 1 cup salsa mild or hot (if you're feeling frisky)
  • 1 pack of hard taco shells (usually come in a 12 pack)
  • 1 medium tomato, chopped
  • 1 cup of shredded cheddar cheese
  1. Cook beef in a large skillet for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally until brown
  2. Stir salsa into beef, heat to boiling and stirr constantly; reduce heat to medium-low.
  3. Heat those yellow hard taco shells as directed
