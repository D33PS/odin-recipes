Manhattan Cocktail

Delicious Manhattan Cocktail.

Don't have to be a pro to make this one

The black cherries compliment this drink beautifully..

Ingredients
  • 2 ounces your favourite whisky or bourbon
  • 1 ounce of sweet vermouth
  • 2 dash of Angostura bitters
  • 1 dash of orange bitters
  1. Add whisky or bourbon
  2. Add into a chilled coupe
  3. Garnish with a brandied cherry
