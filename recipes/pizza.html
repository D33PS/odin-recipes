Pizza

Hot and fresh pepperoni and cheese pizza.

This recipe takes no time at all to make, it is something simple you can make after a long day at work.

Best part of making this pizza, you can pair it with your favourite drink... although an ice cold brew is recommended.

Ingredients
  • Fresh or frozen dough
  • Pepperoni (either hot or mild)
  • Canned pizza sauce
  • Lastly some fresh mozzarella cheese
  1. Preheat the oven to 450 degrees
  2. Oil the pan with olive oil
  3. Shred the mozzarella cheese
  4. Sauce the dough
  5. Sprinkle the mozzarella on top of the sauce
  6. Put enough pepperoni to cover the pizza
  7. Toss it in the overn for 25 mins
  8. Enjoy!
